The Science of Reading Research in Action
Decoding with Language Comprehension Results in Reading Comprehension
Benchmark Phonics is designed to reflect current and confirmed research on learning to read and teaching reading.
  • Explicit and Systematic Phonics Instruction
  • Frequent Application to Reading and Writing
  • Built-In Review and Repetition Cycle
  • Regularly Builds Vocabulary and Content Knowledge
Hollis Scarborough, 2001. Connecting early language and literacy to later reading (dis)abilities: Evidence, theory and practice. In S. Newman & D. Dickinson (Eds.), Handbook of Early Literacy Research. Used by permission of Guilford Press.
Why Structured Literacy Works
Structured literacy explicitly teaches phonics and word study skills and strategies. These benefit all students, but especially students with dyslexia.
