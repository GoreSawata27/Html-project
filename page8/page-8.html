Decodable Texts
Beautifully Designed for Engagement, Purposefully Designed for Application
My Reading and Writing with Embedded Decodable Texts
Every week, students read an I Read selection in their My Reading and Writing student consumable book. This text is a decodable text that incorporates the primary skill, as well as previously taught skills, high-frequency words, and content area vocabulary.
Decodable Texts for Application and Practice
Multiple decodable texts are available for each week of instruction. These decodable texts provide opportunities for students to apply new and previously taught skills and high-frequency words, and to connect to the knowledge-building topic of the unit.
Black-and-White Decodables for Each Student’s Hands
Black-and-white versions of each decodable text are provided so that every student receives a copy for application and practice.
